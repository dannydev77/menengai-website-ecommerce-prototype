Our Heritage

1988

Menengai Oil Refinaries Ltd began in 1988 by Mr.P.D.Shap and his father Mr.D.K.Shah. The organization started from humble beginnings in upholstery business the referred to as Menengai Drapers Ltd.

1990

Later, in 1990 three brothers jointly registered the Menengai Soap Factory Ltd.

1994

The manufacture of soap was a successful venture and in 1994 the company ventured into manufacture of edible cooking oil thus the birth of Menengai Oil Refinaries Ltd.

vision

At Menengai Oil, we aspire to be at the heart of all our stakeholders, we believe in shared success. We envision having our products available in every home in East and Central Africa. We are driven by ambition and by the satisfaction of our consumers. Quality experiences by our consumers are central to our success.

mission

Our mission is to be the number one producer of home care goods and consumables in East Africa. We aspire to be the heartbeat of every home in our target market. As we grow, our mission is to grow those around us, giving back to our society is core to our success.
